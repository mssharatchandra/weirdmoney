# WYRD memepool — post formats

The writer picks ONE format per post. Never use the same format twice in a row.
Each has a skeleton and an exemplar. `heat` = how well it's landing (1-5); bump by
hand as you see what hits. The number always does the comedy.

---

### 1. deadpan stat drop  ·  heat 5  ·  the workhorse
The pure absurd fact + odds + dollars, stated flat. no framing.
> there is currently $340,000 riding on whether a bird lands on the pope. yes is at 12%.

### 2. brother is NOT ___  ·  heat 4  ·  for delusional YES odds
When people are betting big on something clearly not happening.
> $1.2m says aliens get confirmed this year. brother. brother is NOT getting confirmed this year.

### 3. incredulous repetition  ·  heat 5  ·  rage-bait, safe kind
Repeat the number like you can't believe it. great for sports win-probs.
> the market gives your team a 4% chance. four. percent. anyway.

### 4. the money vs you  ·  heat 4  ·  poll energy
State what the crowd thinks vs what the money thinks. bait the disagreement.
> you think it's a coin flip. the money says 23%. one of you is lying and it's costing you.

### 5. per my last email  ·  heat 3  ·  formal-register clash
Corporate/formal tone wrapped around something unhinged.
> per my last email, there is $214,000 riding on a bird. please advise.

### 6. they're betting on EVERYTHING  ·  heat 4  ·  screenshot energy
Pure disbelief at the scope of what has a market.
> they made a market for this. they made a market for THIS. $88k in. 😭

### 7. somebody knows something  ·  heat 4  ·  line movement (needs a price move)
Only when you have real movement data.
> "___ happens by friday" just went 8% → 31% in three hours. somebody knows something.

### 8. the india special  ·  heat 5  ·  forbidden fruit (india-relevant markets)
Lean into the ban. the whole readership feels this one.
> this one's illegal for you to touch. doesn't stop the internet. $Xk says [thing]. [odds].

### 9. greentext micro-story  ·  heat 3  ·  whale / narrative markets
> be me. see market. "$500k on whether [X]". odds: [Y]%. close laptop. reopen. still there.

### 10. daily top-5 thread  ·  heat 5  ·  once a day, the follow driver
"top 5 most unhinged bets on the internet right now 🧵" then one per post, weirdest last.
